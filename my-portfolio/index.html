2024 - 2025

About project

  • Developed a two-stage machine learning pipeline for automated job title identification from unstructured online job advertisements.
  • Implemented BERT-based sector classification to categorize job ads into predefined domains such as IT, Healthcare, Education, and Finance.
  • Used TF-IDF, Word2Vec, and BERT embeddings along with similarity-based matching techniques like Word Mover’s Distance (WMD) for accurate job title prediction.
